\chapter*{Abstract}

Write a short abstract here (5--10 lines). Summarize the goal, method, and key result.

\chapter*{Acknowledgements}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Acknowledgements}
Optional: thank people, funding, etc.

% ---------- Main matter ----------
\clearpage
\pagenumbering{arabic}

\chapter{Introduction}
Explain the context, motivation, and what the report covers.

\section{Problem Statement}
What exactly are you trying to solve/answer?

\section{Contributions}
A short bullet list can help:
\begin{itemize}
  \item Contribution 1
  \item Contribution 2
  \item Contribution 3
\end{itemize}

\chapter{Background / Theory}
Define important concepts and cite sources \citep{knuth1984texbook}.

\chapter{Methodology}
Describe data, tools, assumptions, and procedure.

\section{Data}
Where does it come from? What format? Any cleaning?

\section{Approach}
Explain your steps, algorithms, or process.

\chapter{Results}
Present results with figures and tables.

\section{Example Figure}
\begin{figure}[h]
  \centering
  \includegraphics[width=0.75\textwidth]{images/example.png}
  \caption{Example figure caption.}
  \label{fig:example}
\end{figure}

Reference it like \cref{fig:example}.

\section{Example Table}
\begin{table}[h]
  \centering
  \begin{tabular}{lrr}
    \toprule
    Item & Value A & Value B \\
    \midrule
    Foo  & 10      & 12 \\
    Bar  & 7       & 9 \\
    \bottomrule
  \end{tabular}
  \caption{Example table caption.}
  \label{tab:example}
\end{table}

Reference it like \cref{tab:example}.

\chapter{Discussion}
Interpret results: what do they mean, limitations, what surprised you, why it matters.

\chapter{Conclusion}
Summarize the key points and suggest future work.
